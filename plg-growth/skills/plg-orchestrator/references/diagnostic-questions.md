# PLG Diagnostic Questionnaire

Use this questionnaire to rapidly assess a product's PLG situation. Ask conversationally — do not dump all questions at once. Adapt based on what the user has already shared.

## Round 1: Core Context (Always Ask)

### 1.1 Product & Market
- **What does your product do?** (One sentence: who is the user, what problem does it solve?)
- **Who is your target user?** (Individual contributor? Manager? Executive? Developer? Designer?)
- **What is the buying dynamic?** (User = buyer? User != buyer? Committee purchase?)
- **What market/category are you in?** (Established category? Emerging? Creating new?)
- **How many competitors exist?** (Dominant player? Fragmented? Greenfield?)

### 1.2 Current Growth Model
- **How do you acquire customers today?** (Outbound sales? Inbound marketing? Word of mouth? Partnerships? Product virality?)
- **What is your primary revenue model?** (Subscription SaaS? Usage-based? Transactional? Marketplace? Freemium? Ad-supported? Hybrid?)
- **Do you have a free tier or trial today?** (If yes: what kind? How long? What's included?)
- **What does your signup flow look like?** (Self-serve? Demo required? Contact sales? Gated? Ungated?)

### 1.3 Stage & Traction
- **What stage are you at?** (Pre-launch? Early traction? Growth? Scale? Mature?)
- **Do you have product-market fit signals?** (Revenue? Retention? Sean Ellis test? Usage patterns?)
- **Approximate ARR or MRR?** (Order of magnitude is fine: <$100K, $100K-$1M, $1M-$10M, $10M+)
- **How many active users/customers?** (Order of magnitude)

## Round 2: Depth (Ask if Needed)

### 2.1 Team & Organization
- **How large is your team?** (Total, and specifically product/engineering/growth)
- **Do you have a growth team?** (Dedicated? Part of product? No dedicated function?)
- **Who owns acquisition today?** (Marketing? Sales? Product? Nobody specifically?)
- **Engineering capacity for growth work?** (Dedicated? Shared? None?)

### 2.2 Data Maturity
- **What analytics tools do you use?** (Product analytics? Revenue analytics? Experimentation?)
- **Can you track user-level behavior from signup to revenue?** (Full funnel visibility? Gaps?)
- **Do you run experiments/A/B tests?** (Regularly? Occasionally? Never?)
- **Do you have cohort data?** (Retention curves? Revenue cohorts? Activation cohorts?)

### 2.3 Current Metrics (if available)
- **Acquisition:** Monthly signups? Traffic to signup conversion rate?
- **Activation:** What % of signups reach "aha moment"? What is the aha moment?
- **Retention:** D7/D30/D90 retention? Or monthly retention?
- **Monetisation:** Free-to-paid conversion rate? ARPU/ARPA? LTV? CAC?
- **Expansion:** Net revenue retention? Expansion revenue %?

### 2.4 Biggest Pain Point
- **What is the #1 growth challenge you are facing right now?**
- **What have you tried that did not work?**
- **If you could fix one metric, which would it be?**

## How to Use Responses

### Quick Classification
Based on Round 1 answers, classify into one of these starting points:

| Signal | Starting Skill |
|--------|---------------|
| Unsure if PLG is right | plg-readiness |
| PLG-viable but no free offering yet | acquisition-model-selector |
| Has free offering, unclear where to focus | plg-revenue-analysis |
| Clear funnel bottleneck | Domain skill (acquisition/activation/retention/monetisation) |
| Has PLG but wants to optimize | plg-revenue-analysis → domain skill |
| Pre-PMF, exploring PLG | plg-readiness (deep assessment including decision-driver research) |

### Red Flags That Suggest Non-PLG Primary
- Average deal size > $50K/year AND buyer is always executive committee
- Product requires weeks of implementation before any value
- No way for user to self-serve any part of the journey
- Highly regulated environment where procurement is mandatory
- Product value only emerges with organization-wide adoption (no individual use case)

Note: Red flags do not mean "no PLG." They mean PLG is likely a secondary motion. Recommend hybrid approach via plg-readiness assessment.

### Information Gaps to Flag
If user cannot answer these, note as key unknowns in the diagnostic brief:
- Activation rate (they do not know the aha moment)
- Free-to-paid conversion (they do not track it)
- Retention curve shape (they do not have cohort data)
- CAC by channel (they do not measure it)

These gaps themselves are diagnostic: they suggest data setup should be an early priority.
